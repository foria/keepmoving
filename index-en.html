Keep Moving
We create links through music.

We design the musical programme of wonderful places and collaborate in the musical identity of recognized brand

We are a creative agency with a curious spirit and desire to innovate, we prioritize human relationships, a close contact with clients and the passion for good music.

MUSICAL PROGRAMMING

Our focus is on decoding the essence of the city through its artists. Our restless search for talent and creativity turns our agency into a great link between companies, brands and original projects.

CUSTOMIZED MIX

We create a customized playlist, we carefully select each track to create a unique mix. We assign a selector to each project so that it transmits the essence of the brand without using algorithms, building personality and transforming customers into followers.

EVENTS

When we join an event we work to reflect the brand’s spirit in every detail. We design the ideal soundtrack for each moment, accompanying the brands that choose us with a good rhythm.

COMMUNICATION

We support the communication strategy of the client to boost visibility of the musical events and programmes. High-quality photos, press releases and customized designs to transform communication assets into brand ambassadors along with music.

stories

Vermuts al Soul

Hotel 1898

Soul Hotel 1898 is located on the emblematic Ramblas of Barcelona in a magnificent 19th century mansion. The “Vermouths Soul” events take place on Sundays on La Isabela terrace and have brought together some of the best DJs and vinyl collectors in the city in a unique placement and atmosphere.

Old Fashioned de Verbena

Monument Hotel

The Verbena restaurant at Monument Hotel has got some of themost stunning views on Passeig de Gracia. In collaboration with Dry Martini we created the Old Fashioned sessions where great classics of Soul, Funk and Boogie become the ideal soundtrack to enjoy this legendary cocktail.

Seventy Direct

Seventy Hotel

Seventy is a unique concept that fuses the warmth of a Mediterranean guest house and the excitement of a multipurpose space. Our collaboration with this great project consists of designing part of the extensive musical programme in order to offer a unique experience to guests and authentic Barcelonians.

360o Sessions

Barceló Raval

The Barceló Raval is an urban hotel that stands out from the rest for its innovative design and its particular location, in one of the most multicultural neighborhoods in the city. The 360º Sessions bring together the most relevant DJs in the city to join us in the magical moment of the sunset over Tibidabo mountain.

Vegan party

Ben & Jerry's

Among the brands with which we have collaborated, Ben & Jerry's is one of our favorites, the fun and originality of their advertising actions motivate and amuse us. We collaborated in the launch event of their vegan range, where music and almonds were essential in a party full of non-dairy fun.

The Top

Gallery Hotel

The Top terrace located on the roof of Gallery Hotel in Barcelona's Eixample is a meeting point for those looking to enjoy the good weather in the city centre. The Top Live and The Top Sessions series have been a classic for more than five years.

She’s The Boss

The Corner Hotel

On the ground floor of The Corner Hotel we find the Hall & Lounge, a different open space, a meeting point for guests and locals. In the She's The Boss sessions, girls bring energy and elegance to the lobby afternoons with sessions where Funk, House or Soul predominate.

Noche Celeste

Bodega Torres

A series of actions designed especially to enjoy the starry nights of summer, the same ones that watch and contemplate the birth of Celeste Crianza. In collaboration with the Plateselector agency and Bodegas Torres we programmed the live music performances that entertained these unforgettable evenings.
